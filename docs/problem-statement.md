# Problem Statement

At INES Ruhengeri, event registration and attendance management are mostly handled using manual methods such as paper lists, WhatsApp messages, or verbal confirmations. These methods are slow, unreliable, and prone to errors.

Event organizers often face problems such as duplicate registrations, missing attendee records, and the inability to verify whether a student is truly registered for an event. During event check-in, confirming attendance becomes difficult and time-consuming because there is no unique ticket reference for each attendee.

Students also suffer from this system because they do not receive official confirmation after registering. They may arrive at an event only to find their names missing from attendance lists, which leads to confusion and disputes.

The INES Event Ticket Generator is a full-stack web application designed to solve these problems. The system allows attendees to register online and receive a unique ticket code. Event organizers can manage registrations, verify tickets during check-in, and monitor attendance through a dashboard. This digital solution reduces errors, improves efficiency, and ensures transparent event management at INES.
